Trust Structure Details

Master Trust

+

Primary Purpose

Serves as the foundational structure for all family wealth, providing ultimate asset protection and coordinating distributions to subsidiary trusts.

Key Features

  • Irrevocable structure for maximum protection
  • Sophisticated distribution mechanisms
  • Professional trustee oversight
  • Tax-efficient design

Governance

Managed by independent professional trustees with family investment committee oversight and advisory capacity.

Distribution Policy

Strategic distributions to subsidiary trusts based on established criteria, family needs, and long-term wealth preservation goals.

Family Trust

+

Primary Purpose

Direct support for family members' health, education, maintenance, and support (HEMS) while preserving capital for future generations.

Beneficiary Structure

  • Current family members and spouses
  • Future descendants
  • Discretionary distributions
  • Educational support provisions

Distribution Guidelines

Supports family members' lifestyle, education, healthcare, and approved investments while encouraging personal responsibility and achievement.

Protection Features

Spendthrift provisions, creditor protection, and divorce protection mechanisms to safeguard family wealth from external claims.

Operating Trusts

+

Business Operations Trust

Holds active business interests, operating companies, and entrepreneurial ventures. Provides liability protection while maintaining operational flexibility.

Real Estate Trust

Manages family real estate portfolio including primary residences, vacation properties, and investment real estate holdings.

Securities Trust

Handles liquid investment portfolio including public equities, bonds, and traditional investment vehicles with professional management oversight.

Alternative Investments Trust

Specialized vehicle for private equity, hedge funds, venture capital, and other alternative investment strategies requiring different risk profiles.

Beneficiary Trusts

+

Generation 1 (Current)

Provides for current generation family members with emphasis on lifestyle maintenance, business opportunities, and philanthropic activities.

Generation 2 (Next)

Supports next generation development through education funding, career support, and gradual wealth transition with built-in responsibility mechanisms.

Generation 3+ (Future)

Perpetual trust structure ensuring wealth preservation and family values transmission to unborn generations with adaptive distribution mechanisms.

Protection Mechanisms

Each trust includes spendthrift provisions, creditor protection, incentive clauses, and governance structures appropriate for the beneficiary generation's needs.
