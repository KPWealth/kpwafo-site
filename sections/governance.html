Asset Class Strategies

Public Equities (35%)

Diversified portfolio of global equities focusing on high-quality companies with strong governance and sustainable competitive advantages.

  • Large-cap core holdings (60%)
  • International developed markets (25%)
  • Emerging markets exposure (15%)

Fixed Income (25%)

Conservative bond portfolio emphasizing capital preservation and stable income generation across varying interest rate environments.

  • Government and sovereign bonds (50%)
  • Investment-grade corporate bonds (35%)
  • Inflation-protected securities (15%)

Alternative Investments (20%)

Diversified alternatives providing uncorrelated returns and enhanced portfolio resilience through economic cycles.

  • Private equity and venture capital (40%)
  • Hedge fund strategies (35%)
  • Commodities and natural resources (25%)

Real Estate (15%)

Direct property ownership and real estate investment trusts providing inflation protection and diversification benefits.

  • Premium residential properties (50%)
  • Commercial real estate (30%)
  • REITs and real estate funds (20%)
